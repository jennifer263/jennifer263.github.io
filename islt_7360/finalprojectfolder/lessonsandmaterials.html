Unit 1-Our Nation's First Presidency and Government

Goals

Students will be able to explain how the nation's leaders organized the new government.

Essential Questions

  • How did early American leaders respond to the social, political and economic challenges facing our new nation?
  • How might you make connections between Washington's decisions and emerging political parties?
  • How did the laws, policies, and processes of the government affect individuals? How did they effect groups?
  • What might society look like if Federalism were present?
  • What might a society look like if popular sovereignty is prevalent?

Vocabulary

  • correleation
  • chronological
  • popular sovereignty
  • federalism
